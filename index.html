About Me

I am a Freelance Writer from Kenya with a deep interest in data analytics, data science, and data engineering. This passion was sparked during my undergraduate studies in Microbiology and Biotechnology when I first encountered Biostatistics and this led me along an unconventional learning route. Given that I had no prior or formal training in programming, I opted for online courses and platforms as well as reading books and articles that taught me the basics. This meant countless hours of trial and error.

I attribute my change of career path to the pandemic which pushed me to freelance writing where I got the chance to learn about data analysis in my free time. This culminated in earning a Data Science Professional certification from DataCamp which strengthened my basic understanding of Data Science and the route to take in my learning process.

Currently, I apply my skills to get valuable insights and solutions from data while continuing to share my experience through writing.
